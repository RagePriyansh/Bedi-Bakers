Give your taste buds a treat at Bedi Bakers! Since 1955. We've been keeping the amazing people of Moradabad well-fed and happy with our scrumptious baked goods. Our original creations and signature products are sure to exceed all of your expectations.

We're passionate bakers with a commitment to our community and we plan on continuing this tradition for years to come. we welcome you to come on in and have a bite.
